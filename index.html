Moms On a Mission is a 501(c)(3) non-profit public charity based in Dupo, IL, serving communities across Illinois and Missouri with love and compassion.

Our Mission

We are a group of everyday people making a difference everyday. With no paid staff, we are 100% donation-driven and ALWAYS GIVING. We lovingly serve our homeless and displaced friends, foster children and families, veterans, teens, low-income families, disabled individuals, and our elderly.

Who We Serve

Homeless and displaced individuals, foster children and families, veterans, teens, low-income families, disabled people, and our elderly community members.

What We Provide

Everything from food to clothing, hygiene products and shoes to prom dresses and window AC units - meeting needs wherever we find them.

The Pay-or-Pray

Our nonprofit donation-only thrift shop where everyone is welcome, whether they can pay or just need to pray.

How It All Started

"I don't have much, monetary speaking...as an adult, I never have. I've had to work as hard as anyone to keep my head above water. I carried this as a burden for a long time. It seemed that as soon as I got ahead, I'd be pulled back in...only deeper. It was 'so unfair.'

I was so caught up in feeling sorry for myself that I was missing what was happening.... I wasn't burdened...I was BLESSED!!

There was always somebody there with an extended hand and a smile. I've never gone without for long. Be it food, utilities, transportation, clothes or just a 'you're going to be alright'...even a washer and dryer.... I've been helped by SO many.

I still need help, as we all do but I've decided it's time for me to Pay It Forward and I became a Mom On a Mission."

- Whenever you fall, pick something up.
