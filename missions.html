Our Missions

Discover the many ways we serve our communities across Illinois and Missouri, meeting needs wherever we find them.

Serving With Love

We are based out of Dupo, IL but we serve MANY areas in both Illinois and Missouri. With no paid staff, we are 100% donation-driven and ALWAYS GIVING.

Homeless & Displaced Friends

We lovingly serve our homeless and displaced friends on the streets, providing food, clothing, hygiene products, and compassionate support.

Foster Children & Families

Supporting foster children and foster families with essential items, clothing, and resources to help them thrive.

Veterans

Honoring and assisting those who served our country with supplies, support, and the respect they deserve.

Low Income Families

Helping families stretch their resources with food assistance, clothing, household items, and even window AC units.

Teens & Youth

From school supplies to prom dresses, we help teens navigate life's special moments and everyday challenges.

Disabled & Elderly

Providing support and dignity to our disabled and elderly community members with necessities and companionship.
